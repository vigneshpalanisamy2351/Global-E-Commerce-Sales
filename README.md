# Global-E-Commerce-Sales
This project will tells the sales of Global E-Commerence 
Global E-Commerence Analysis Dataset is [E-Commerence](https://github.com/vigneshpalanisamy2351/Global-E-Commerce-Sales/blob/main/global_ecommerce_sales.csv)

---

**1.Key Performance Metrics** - Total Sales:(479.95K)This represents the overall revenue generated from all transactions - It is calculated by adding all sales values using the SUM of Sales.Total Orders: 2K This indicates the total number of transactions/orders placed by customers.Strong revenue combined with a healthy profit margin indicates an efficient sales strategy. The business is maintaining profitability while scaling order volume, suggesting good cost control and pricing optimization.

---

**2.Regional Performance Insights** North America: Highest revenue contributor,Europe: Stable and consistent performance,Asia Pacific: Emerging growth region,Middle East & Africa / South America: Lower contribution Revenue is concentrated in North America. Business expansion strategies should prioritize Asia Pacific for growth, while maintaining dominance in established markets

---

**3.Time-Based Sales Trends** Monthly Analysis:Shows fluctuations across the year,Peak Months: October–November (highest sales),Low Period: Mid-year dip (around August),Seasonal demand patterns highlight the need for inventory planning and marketing campaigns during peak periods. Mid-year strategies can help stabilize revenue.

---

**4.Customer Segment Insights** Consumer Segment: Highest revenue contributor,Corporate Segment: Moderate contribution,Home Office: Lowest share,
The business is highly dependent on individual consumers. Diversifying focus toward corporate clients can improve revenue stability and long-term contracts.

---

**5.Customer Experience & Payments** Payment Methods,Bank Transfer,Cash on Delivery,Credit Card,PayPal.Multiple payment options enhance customer convenience and improve conversion rates. Digital payment adoption supports faster transactions and scalability.

---

**6.Data Engineering & Dashboard Design** Custom Measures: Created using DAX (Total Sales, Profit, Order Count), Interactive Filters: Slicers for Year, Profit Status, Payment Mode,Visual Design: KPI cards, maps, bar charts, and donut charts

The dashboard acts as a single source of truth, enabling quick and data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders.
